 define_function "max", max
   xx dup2, greater_than?
   xx one?,false?branch,3
   xx drop,end
   xx swap
   xx drop
   xx end 


define_function "min", min
   xx dup2, less_than?
   xx one?,false?branch,3
   xx drop,end
   xx swap
   xx drop
   xx end



说明：输入两个数  然后输入max或min，可输出较大值或较小值
